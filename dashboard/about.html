About the Project

Say hello to BeeSmart, a new smart connected device made to empower beekeepers with seamless remote monitoring of beehive health. BeeSmart is outfitted with four highly precise load sensors that accurately gauge hive weight. Additionally, it features two advanced temperature probes, offering beekeepers the flexibility to insert them directly into their hive at the location of their choosing. BeeSmart is powered by the incredible Arduino MKR 1010, serving as the brains of the device. It seamlessly interfaces with these sensors, capturing vital hive data in real-time and integrating with the MatLab API. This allows the invaluable information to be effortlessly stored and presented in visually captivating displays.
